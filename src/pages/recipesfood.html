RECIPE OF THE WEEK!

Grilled Chicken Club Wrap

246 cals | 1 serving

Ingredients
1 Tbsp. Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Light Mayonnaise
1 large flour tortillas
4 oz. grilled chicken
2 slices tomatoes
1 slice red onion
2 romaine lettuce leaves

Steps
1. Evenly spread the mayonnaise
2. Add the ingredients
3. Roll and fold it
4. Enjoy the food!
