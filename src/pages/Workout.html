Workout

To be healthy and fit, apart from maintaining our diet, we also have to balance it with exercise.

Exercise is a physical activity carried out regularly with the aim of improving health, physical fitness and overall well-being. The types of exercise vary, from yoga which focuses on balancing the mind and body, cardio to increase your heart rate, or intensive exercise such as lifting weights to train muscles.

Start with a sport that suits your abilities and interests, and enjoy the benefits!

Yoga

Yoga is a holistic practice originating in ancient India that combines body movement, breath control, meditation, and philosophical principles to achieve balance between mind, body, and spirit. Types of yoga vary, including Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Kundalini, and more, each with a different emphasis on physical movement, breathing, and meditation.

Yoga can be beneficial for a variety of health conditions, including stress, anxiety, depression, insomnia, and back pain

Cardio

Cardio for cardiovascular refers to physical exercise that increases heart rate and breathing, burns calories, and improves cardiovascular fitness. This usually involves aerobic activities such as running, cycling, swimming, or jumping rope.

Cardio can be beneficial for a variety of illnesses, including heart disease, heart disease, high blood pressure, and obesity. It can also improve mood, energy, and sleep quality.

Weightlifting

Weight lifting, or strength training, is a type of physical exercise that involves using additional resistance to train and strengthen the muscles of the body. Its primary goal is to increase strength, muscle mass, and overall fitness. Types of weight lifting include Free weight, Machine exercise, Body-weight Exercise.

Weight lifting can be beneficial for various health conditions, including osteoporosis, weight loss, diabetes, and metabolism improvement. It can also help improve body posture, balance, and bone health.
